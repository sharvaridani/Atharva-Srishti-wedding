# 💍 Atharva ❤️ Srishti Wedding Website

A beautiful and responsive wedding invitation website created for the wedding celebration of **Atharva Dani** and **Srishti Garg**.

The website serves as a digital wedding invitation where guests can explore the couple's story, event schedule, venue details, RSVP, accommodation information, FAQs, photos, and much more.

---

## 🌸 Features

- 🏡 Elegant Landing Page
- ❤️ Bride & Groom Introduction
- 📖 Our Love Story
- 📅 Wedding Schedule
- 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Meet Our Families
- 📸 Photo Gallery
- 🏨 Travel & Accommodation Details
- 🚗 Parking & Transportation Information
- 📝 RSVP / Confirm Attendance
- ❓ Frequently Asked Questions
- ⏳ Live Wedding Countdown Timer
- 📱 Fully Responsive Design

---

## 🛠️ Built With

- CSS3

