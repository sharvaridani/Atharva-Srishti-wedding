Frequently Asked Questions

Some extra info for folks

What's the weather like?

Pleasant winter weather with cool mornings and evenings.
Average High: 28°C (82°F)
Average Low: 13°C (55°F)

December in Murtizapur offers beautiful weather for celebrating with family and friends. We recommend carrying a light jacket or shawl for the evening events.

How do I get to the venue?

Click here to return to the Home Page for the Google Maps location.
The wedding celebrations will be held at:

Salasar Resort
Murtizapur, District Akola,
Maharashtra, India

Is parking available? Is it free?

Yes! Complimentary parking is available for all our guests within the resort premises. Our volunteers will be present to guide you, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free parking experience.

Will the ceremonies be indoors or outdoors?

Our celebrations will take place across beautifully decorated indoor and outdoor spaces at Salasar Resort. Every venue has been thoughtfully arranged to ensure your comfort while enjoying the festivities.

When is the RSVP deadline?

Kindly confirm your attendance on or before 15th November 2026.

We request you to RSVP through our Confirm Attendance page so we can make all the necessary arrangements for your stay and hospitality.

Is there a dress code?

Absolutely! Each celebration has its own unique dress code to make the festivities even more vibrant.

Please click here to view the complete event schedule and dress code details.

What time should I arrive?

We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before the start of each event so everyone can be comfortably seated before the celebrations begin.

Is accommodation available?

Accommodation has been arranged for invited outstation guests. If your stay has been confirmed, our team will share all accommodation details with you before the wedding.

Will transportation be provided?

Yes. Transportation will be arranged for guests staying at the designated accommodation, ensuring convenient travel between the hotel and the wedding venue.

Can children attend the wedding?

Absolutely! Our wedding is a celebration for the entire family. We look forward to celebrating this special occasion with guests of all ages.

Can I take photos and videos?

Of course! We would love for you to capture and share the beautiful moments. Feel free to take photographs and videos, and don't forget to tag us when posting your memories.

Any other questions?

We'd be delighted to help! Please contact us via phone, WhatsApp, email, or social media if you have any questions regarding the wedding. We look forward to celebrating our special day with you!
