Travel

By Air – Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar International Airport, Nagpur (NAG)

Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar International Airport is the nearest major airport to Salasar Resort, Murtizapur. The airport is approximately 145 km from the resort and offers domestic connectivity to major Indian cities. Visitors can hire taxis or rental cars from the airport to reach the venue.

Click the links below to help book your transportation to the venue.

Uber | Ola Cabs | Airport Taxi

By Train – Murtizapur Junction Railway Station

Murtizapur Junction Railway Station (MZR) is the nearest railway station to Salasar Resort. It is well connected to major cities including Mumbai, Nagpur, Akola, Pune, and Bhusawal. The resort is approximately 8–10 km from the station and can be reached by taxi or auto-rickshaw.

By Road

Salasar Resort is conveniently accessible via the Mumbai–Nagpur Highway (NH-53). The resort is well connected by road from nearby cities such as Akola, Amravati, Nagpur, and Washim. Private taxis, self-drive vehicles, and state transport buses are available for easy access.
