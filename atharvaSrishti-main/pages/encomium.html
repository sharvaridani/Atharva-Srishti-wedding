Two Families, One Celebration

Where Two Homes Become One Family

Every wedding is more than the union of two hearts—it's the beautiful coming together of two families, two traditions, and countless memories waiting to be created.

Today, the Dani and Garg families joyfully welcome you to celebrate this special beginning with love, laughter, and blessings.

The Dani Family

Rooted in the warmth of Murtizapur, the Dani family believes in celebrating every occasion with togetherness, traditions, and unconditional love. Their home has always been filled with laughter, cherished memories, and values that have shaped generations.

Mr. Mukund Dani and Mrs. Jayashree Dani have lovingly raised Atharva with kindness, integrity, and compassion. Their unwavering support, guidance, and blessings have helped him become the person he is today. As they welcome Srishti into their family, their hearts are filled with gratitude and happiness for this beautiful new beginning.

"Family is where life begins and love never ends."

The Garg Family

The Garg family has always believed that love, respect, and togetherness are life's greatest treasures. Every celebration has been a reflection of their warmth, generosity, and strong family values.

Mr. Vijaykumar Garg and Mrs. Rishika Garg have nurtured Srishti with endless love, encouragement, and the confidence to dream big. Watching their daughter begin this beautiful journey with Atharva is a moment of immense joy and pride for the entire family.

"Two families, countless blessings, and one beautiful celebration of love."

Together Forever

Today, the Dani Family and the Garg Family come together not only to celebrate the wedding of Atharva and Srishti, but to embrace a new relationship built on love, respect, and lifelong togetherness.

Your presence, blessings, and good wishes make this celebration even more meaningful. We look forward to creating beautiful memories with all of you as we witness the beginning of their forever.

"Two families, one celebration, and a lifetime of happiness begins."
