The Bride & Groom

Atharva

Born on 31st August 1998, Atharva is the beloved son of Mrs. Jayashree Dani and Mr. Mukund Dani from the beautiful town of Murtizapur. From the very beginning, he was the cutest little boy with an innocent smile that could brighten anyone's day. A true mumma's boy, Atharva was always known for his caring nature, discipline and obedience. Whether at home, school or among relatives, he was loved for his simplicity, kindness and cheerful personality.

Alongside academics, sports became an inseparable part of his life. Volleyball wasn't just a game—it became his passion, making him one of the finest players in his school. His childhood was filled with laughter, adventures and countless memories shared with his closest friends Sumit Upadhye, Ankit Jaiswal and Kshitij Kundley, friendships that continue to stand strong even today.

After completing his schooling, Atharva moved to Hyderabad for his higher secondary education before pursuing Engineering at Savitribai Phule Pune University. He has also recently completed an MBA in Business Analytics, further strengthening his passion for innovation, technology, and business.

Pune wasn't just where he earned a degree—it became home to friendships that turned into family. Ganesh Kirdak, Pranil Kulkarni and Gogi became his lifelong companions, making every college memory unforgettable.

With determination and ambition, Atharva stepped into the corporate world, where his dedication and perseverance helped him build a successful career. Every milestone became another stepping stone, and his journey continues to inspire everyone around him.

P.S.
If there's a football match on TV or a PUBG game waiting to be played, good luck getting his attention! Between football, gaming, and long conversations with the people he loves, Atharva finds happiness in life's simplest moments.

Then destiny had something extraordinary in store. Through a mutual friend, Atharva met Srishti. What began as a simple introduction blossomed into endless conversations, beautiful memories and a love story built on trust, friendship and understanding. Their engagement marked the beginning of a lifetime promise, and today they stand hand in hand, ready to begin the most beautiful chapter of their lives together.

"Every journey has a destination, and for Atharva, that destination has always been a life filled with love, family, friendship... and now, Srishti."


Srishti

Born on 15th September 2000, Srishti is the beloved daughter of Mr. Vijaykumar Garg and Mrs. Rishika Garg. From her very first smile, she captured everyone's heart with her innocence, kindness, and cheerful nature. A sweet, obedient, and lovable little girl, Srishti always dreamed big while keeping her feet firmly grounded. Her warmth, compassion, and ever-present smile have made her a source of happiness for everyone fortunate enough to know her.

Growing up, Srishti had a unique fascination with horror movies. While most people would hide behind a pillow during the scary scenes, she would be completely engrossed, enjoying every thrilling moment. Her fearless spirit, combined with her caring heart, perfectly reflects the wonderful person she is today.

Srishti completed her schooling in the beautiful city of Chandigarh, where she laid the foundation for her academic journey. Driven by her passion for understanding people and helping them grow, she pursued both her Bachelor's and Master's degree in Psychology from the prestigious Thapar University. She is also currently pursuing an MBA in Human Resources from NMIMS University, Mumbai. During these memorable college years, she found not only knowledge but also a lifelong friendship in Alizha, whose bond has remained one of the greatest gifts of her student life.

Ever ambitious and determined, Srishti always dreamed of building her career in Pune. With dedication and perseverance, she turned that dream into reality by joining Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) as an Executive HR. It was a proud milestone that reflected her hard work, confidence, and commitment to achieving her goals.

P.S.
Don't let her sweet smile fool you! She'll happily choose a horror movie over a romantic one any day. Whether it's spooky movie nights, heartfelt conversations, or making everyone around her smile, Srishti finds joy in life's little moments.

Life had something even more beautiful planned for her. Through a mutual friend, Srishti met Atharva. What started as simple conversations soon turned into endless phone calls, unwavering support, shared dreams, and countless beautiful memories. Their friendship blossomed into a love story built on trust, laughter, understanding, and unconditional love.

Their engagement marked the beginning of a wonderful new chapter, and today they stand hand in hand, surrounded by the love and blessings of their families and friends, ready to begin a lifetime of happiness together.

"A girl with beautiful dreams, a heart full of kindness, and a smile that lights up every room... Srishti found not just the career she dreamed of, but also the love that made every dream complete."


Alright... Here's Our Story

Every beautiful story begins when you least expect it, and ours began in 2024. Through a mutual friend, two strangers crossed paths without knowing that destiny had already written something extraordinary for them.

What started as casual conversations quickly turned into endless phone calls, laughter, and sharing every little detail of life. With every conversation, Atharva and Srishti discovered not only a wonderful friend in each other but also someone who truly understood them. From the very first meeting, there was an unexplainable feeling—a spark that quietly whispered, "This is the one."

As days turned into months, their friendship blossomed into a beautiful love story. Every late-night conversation, every shared dream, every smile, and every moment together strengthened their bond. They stood beside one another through every happiness and every challenge, realizing that life felt complete only when they were together.

Soon, they made the most beautiful promise—to walk through every season of life hand in hand, forever.

When Atharva and Srishti shared their decision with their families, they were welcomed with immense love, blessings, and happiness. With both families coming together wholeheartedly, their beautiful love story found its perfect beginning. Their engagement marked the start of a new chapter, and now the day they've always dreamed of is finally here.

Surrounded by the love of their family and friends, Atharva and Srishti are ready to begin a lifetime of laughter, companionship, adventures, and unconditional love. Their story, which began with friendship in 2024, is now becoming a forever promise.

"Sometimes two hearts are simply meant to find each other. What began with a simple introduction through a mutual friend has become a lifetime of love, friendship, and togetherness."
